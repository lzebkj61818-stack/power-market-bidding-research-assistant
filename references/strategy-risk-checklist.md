# Strategy Risk Checklist

Use this when converting research into candidate bidding scenarios.

## Risk Categories

- market-rule uncertainty
- time-visibility uncertainty
- missing or stale inputs
- execution mapping ambiguity
- operational capacity limits
- exposure concentration
- poor calibration or unstable sizing
- regime shift risk
- overreaction to weak evidence

## Candidate Strategy Requirements

Every candidate should state:

- base case
- why it might work
- what evidence supports it
- what would invalidate it
- how sizing should be limited
- what human judgment is still required

## Red Flags

- recommendation depends on a rule that was not confirmed
- recommendation assumes all inputs are complete without checking
- model score is treated as a direct order
- sizing is written with fake precision
- downside is described vaguely or not at all
